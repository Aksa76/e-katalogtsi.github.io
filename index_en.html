    Certifications

    Our products have received recognition and are certified

    TKDN Certificate

    The TKDN Certificate is an official document issued by the Ministry of Industry (Kemenperin) to prove the percentage of domestic components or services used in a product or service, including raw materials, labor, and production processes.

    Learn More

    SGS ISO 9001:2015 Certificate

    The SGS ISO 9001:2015 Certificate is proof that a company has met international standards for quality management systems, meaning the company has well-managed processes to ensure consistent product or service quality, meet customer requirements, and continuously improve its performance.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our building materials products, ordering, warranty, and other services.

    1. How long is the warranty for Tiger Star Indonesia products?

    We provide a warranty of up to 10 years for spandek and metal roof tile products, and 5 years for bubble foil products. The warranty covers protection against rust and material damage.

    2. Are Tiger Star Indonesia products resistant to extreme weather?

    Yes, all our products are specifically designed for Indonesian weather conditions. Our materials have special coatings that are resistant to UV, acid rain, and extreme temperature changes.

    3. Is delivery service available throughout Indonesia?

    Yes, we serve deliveries to all regions of Indonesia. For the Jabodetabek area, free shipping is available with a minimum order. For other cities, shipping costs are adjusted according to location and order volume.

    4. What about the availability of product colors and sizes?

    We provide various standard and custom color options. For sizes, they can be adjusted according to your project needs. Please consult with our marketing team for further details.

    5. Are Tiger Star Indonesia products certified?

    Yes, all our products are certified with TKDN and ISO 9001:2015. We guarantee material quality and standards in accordance with applicable regulations in Indonesia.
